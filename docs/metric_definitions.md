# Revenue Metrics Definitions

This document explains the main revenue metrics used in this project.

## MRR — Monthly Recurring Revenue

MRR shows the total recurring revenue generated during a specific month.

## Paid Users

Paid Users shows the number of unique users who made a payment during a specific month.

## ARPPU — Average Revenue Per Paid User

ARPPU shows the average revenue generated by one paying user.

Formula:

Revenue / Paid Users

## New Paid Users

New Paid Users shows the number of users who made their first payment in a specific month.

## New MRR

New MRR shows the revenue generated by users who made their first payment in a specific month.

## Churned Users

Churned Users shows users who paid in the previous month but did not pay in the next month.

## Churned Revenue

Churned Revenue shows revenue lost from users who stopped paying.

## Churn Rate

Churn Rate shows the percentage of paying users who stopped paying.

## Revenue Churn Rate

Revenue Churn Rate shows the percentage of revenue lost because of churned users.

## Expansion MRR

Expansion MRR shows additional revenue from existing users who paid more than in the previous month.

## Contraction MRR

Contraction MRR shows lost revenue from existing users who paid less than in the previous month.
